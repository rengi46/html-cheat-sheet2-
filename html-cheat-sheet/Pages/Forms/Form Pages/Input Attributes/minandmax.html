min and max Attributes in HTML

min and max

The min and max attribute specifies the minimum and maximum values for an <input> element.

Tip: Use the min attribute together with the max attribute to create a range of legal values.

Note: The min and max attributes works with the following input types: number, range, date, datetime-local, month, time and week.

Example: 

                    <form action="/action_page.php">

                    <label for="datemax">Enter a date before 1980-01-01:</label>
                    <input type="date" id="datemax" name="datemax" max="1979-12-31"><br><br>

                    <label for="datemin">Enter a date after 2000-01-01:</label>
                    <input type="date" id="datemin" name="datemin" min="2000-01-02"><br><br>

                    <label for="quantity">Quantity (between 1 and 5):</label>
                    <input type="number" id="quantity" name="quantity" min="1" max="5"><br><br>

                    <input type="submit">

                    </form>
